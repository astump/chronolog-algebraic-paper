# Outline of Reworked Chronolog Section

In the original paper, I described Chronolog's more low-level algorithmic details. For this new paper, I want to focus on describing the problems with a direct approach to constraint solving that Chronolog solves. In particular:

- Give an overview of the motivation for Chronolog's design.
- Walk through specific example cases (perhaps 1 or 2 cases) and describe how Chronolog handles them differently from a direct constraint solving approach.
- Walk through the design of the outer loop in the DCS type checker that invokes Chronolog repeatedly.
- Address completeness of the typechecking implementation for the simplified subtyping system.
